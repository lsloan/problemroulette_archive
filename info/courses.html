Courses using Problem Roulette

    Chemistry 130 is a general introduction to the principles of chemistry.

  • MCDB 310

    Molecular, Cellular, and Developmental Biology (MCDB) 310 gives an introduction into the world of biological chemistry.

  • Physics 135

    Physics 135 is the first of a two-course introduction to the physics of life

  • Physics 140

    Physics 140 offers an introduction to classical mechanics, the physics of motion.

  • Physics 235

    Physics 235 is the second of a two-course introduction to the physics of life.

  • Physics 240

    Physics 240 is a continuation of Physics 140, and covers topics in electricity and magnetism.

  • Statistics 250

    Stats 250 is an introduction to statistics and data analysis.
